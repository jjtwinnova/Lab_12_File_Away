"Psych" is a comedy-drama TV series that aired from 2006 to 2014. The show follows Shawn Spencer, a young man with exceptional observation and deduction skills, who pretends to be a psychic to solve crimes alongside his best friend Gus, a pharmaceutical salesman. Together, they work as consultants for the Santa Barbara Police Department, where they use their unique talents to solve a wide variety of cases while also delivering plenty of humor and pop culture references along the way.
